OMELETTE TRACKER
----------------
omelette tracker is a musical instrument and compositional tool designed
primarily for making techno and similar electronic music. It was written
because no other tool I could find could do all the things I wanted to do.


VIEWS
-----
There are 3 main views:
* PATTERN - F1
	Where patterns are written and edited

* INSTRUMENT - F2
	Where instruments are edited

* SONG - F3
	Where patterns are arranged into a full song
